Membership

The Singapore Section of the Combustion Institute (SSCI) welcomes all researchers, engineers, students, and professionals with an interest in the field of combustion. By becoming a member, you join a vibrant community dedicated to advancing combustion science and technology in Singapore and beyond.

Membership Benefits

A member whose subscription is paid up to date shall be entitled to:

  • Receive publications of the Society.
  • Vote at all General Meetings of the Society.
  • Participate in all other activities of the Society.
  • Be part of a network of leading experts in combustion from academia and industry.
  • Receive discounts on registration fees for conferences and workshops organized by the SSCI.
  • Gain access to exclusive resources for news and opportunities in the combustion community.

Membership Fees and Period

The annual membership subscription is valid until September 30th of each year. There is no entrance fee.

  • Regular Member: SGD 30
  • Student/Retired Member: SGD 15
  • Reduced subscription is available for retired members and bona fide full-time students registered with any university in Singapore.

For existing members, annual subscriptions are payable in advance by October 30th each year. Members who fail to settle their arrears for more than one month will automatically cease to be a member.

How to Apply

  1. Download the membership application form: SSCI_Membership_Form.docx
  2. Complete and sign the form.
  3. Please send a scanned copy of the signed form to Prof Huangwei Zhang, Secretary, SSCI. Contact details can be found on our Contact page.
  4. For new members, the annual subscription is payable within thirty (30) days of the approval of your membership.

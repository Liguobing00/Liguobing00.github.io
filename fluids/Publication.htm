Publication

 

 

 

 

 

Authors are invited to submit an abstract (about 200 words) of their proposed contribution by 15 April 2024. The abstract should contain title of the paper, name(s) of author(s) and affiliation(s), full mailing address, fax number and e-mail address of the corresponding author.  The abstract should be submitted to

Prof Cai Qingdong through the e-mail: caiqd@pku.edu.cn

If the paper is accepted, it will be presented at the conference by one of the authors. While there is no restriction on the number of abstracts submitted by any author, each accepted paper must be presented at the conference. The official language of the conference is English.

 

Selected papers presented in the conference will be be considered to be published in Physics of Fluids and Advances in Applied Mathematics and Mechanics.

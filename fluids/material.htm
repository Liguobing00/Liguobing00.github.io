Special Symposium on Advances in Functional Materials

 

 Co-organizer: L. Lu (National University of Singapore), X. P. Li (National University of Singapore) and K. Kitamura (National Institute for Materials Science, Japan)

Functional materials have been received dramatic increase in their demand in research, development and applications. Therefore this Special Symposium aims to assess the current status and to identify future priority and directions in research, design and applications of ferroelectric and ferromagnetic materials, magneto-optical materials, thermoelectric materials, shape memory materials, fuel-cell and battery materials, and other related advanced functional materials. This special symposium also provides an opportunity for promoting mutual interaction, information dissemination and exchange, and fruitful collaborations on advanced, state-of-the-art functional materials research and development being developed.

All the contributions of the papers under this special symposium are by invitation only. For all invited contributors, please kindly send your abstract directly to editor.fmls@nus.edu.sg .

Publication

 

 

 

 

Authors are invited to submit an abstract (about 200 words) of their proposed contribution by 1 March 2008.

The abstract should contain title of the paper, name(s) of author(s) and affiliation(s), full mailing address, fax number and e-mail address of the corresponding author.  The abstract should be submitted to

Miss Zhang Jinfeng through the e-mail: iaaky@nuaa.edu.cn

If the paper is accepted, it will be presented at the conference by one of the authors. While there is no restriction on the number of abstracts submitted by any one author, each accepted paper must be presented at the conference. The official language of the conference is English.

 

Selected papers presented in the conference will be published in the special issue of a SCI-listed journal: Modern Physics Letters B

 

Camera-ready manuscript (no further copyediting will be done) is needed (book trim size : 9.75" x 6.5"). For details of format to prepare your manuscript, please click "Instruction for Manuscript" on the left. Each manuscript is limited to 4 pages.

 

If you use MS word, please click "Template of Word File" to get the template file. If you use Latex, please refer to "Instruction for Manuscript" to get information and template files. The template PDF file is shown in "Template PDF file".
